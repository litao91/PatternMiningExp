Trucks dataset consists of 276 trajectories of 50 trucks delivering concrete to several construction places around Athens metropolitan area in Greece for 33 distinct days. The structure of each record is as follows:

{obj-id, traj-id, date(dd/mm/yyyy), time(hh:mm:ss), lat, lon, x, y}

where (lat, lon) is in WGS84 reference system and (x, y) is in GGRS87 reference system.
