SchoolBuses dataset consists of 145 trajectories of 2 school buses collecting (and delivering) students around Athens metropolitan area in Greece for 108 distinct days. The structure of each record is as follows:

{obj-id, traj-id, date(dd/mm/yyyy), time(hh:mm:ss), lat, lon, x, y}

where (lat, lon) is in WGS84 reference system and (x, y) is in GGRS87 reference system.
